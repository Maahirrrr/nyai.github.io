Security & Trust

Your data belongs to you.
Full stop.

Enterprise-grade security is not a feature at NyAI — it is the foundation. Every architectural decision was made with attorney-client privilege and professional secrecy obligations at its core.

Certifications & Compliance

Meeting the highest standards

🛡️

SOC 2 Type II

Independently audited annually for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. Full audit report available to enterprise prospects under NDA.

🔐

ISO 27001:2022

Certified information security management system. Our ISMS covers all aspects of data processing, access control, incident response, and business continuity.

🇮🇳

DPDP Act 2023

Fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act. We operate as a Data Processor. Users retain full rights to access, correction, and erasure of their data.

🌍

GDPR

Article 27 representative appointed for EU users. Standard Contractual Clauses in place for any cross-border data transfers, though all Indian user data stays in India.

☁️

MeitY Empanelled

Cloud infrastructure empanelled by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. All data is processed within sovereign Indian territory on Mumbai/Pune zone servers.

🔒

Zero Retention Default

By default, no query, document, or session data is retained after a session ends. Conversation history is only stored if you explicitly enable it in your account settings.

Technical Architecture

Built for the sensitivity of legal data

Every NyAI Enterprise deployment runs in an isolated Virtual Private Cloud. No shared infrastructure, no shared models, no shared logs. Your data never touches another client's environment.

We use AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Encryption keys are managed in dedicated Hardware Security Modules and rotated quarterly. Access to production systems requires hardware MFA and is logged immutably.

Our models are hosted entirely on internal infrastructure. We do not route sensitive legal queries through any third-party AI provider including OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google. All inference happens within our controlled environment.

Annual red-team penetration testing is conducted by CERT-In empanelled security firms. We operate a responsible disclosure programme with a 48-hour response SLA for critical vulnerabilities.

🏗️

Private VPC Deployment

Enterprise clients deploy NyAI entirely within their own AWS India or Azure India tenancy. Full network isolation, customer-managed encryption keys, and dedicated compute.

👤

Role-Based Access Control

Granular permissions by matter, client, practice group, and user role. Full SSO and SAML 2.0 integration. Every user action is logged with timestamp and IP address.

📋

Immutable Audit Logs

Every query, document access, export, and configuration change is logged in a write-once, tamper-proof audit trail. Logs are available for internal review and regulatory inspection.

🔄

Disaster Recovery

99.9% uptime SLA with multi-AZ deployment and automated failover. Recovery Point Objective (RPO) of 1 hour and Recovery Time Objective (RTO) of 4 hours for enterprise clients.

🧪

Penetration Testing

Annual third-party red-team exercises by CERT-In empanelled firms. Responsible disclosure programme with public security advisories for all resolved vulnerabilities.

🤝

Contractual Protections

Data Processing Agreements available for all plans. Enterprise clients receive custom DPAs with specific SLAs, breach notification timelines (72-hour default), and audit rights.

Advocate-Client Privilege & Professional Secrecy

NyAI is built in full recognition of Section 126 of the Indian Evidence Act (now reflected in the Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam). Our Zero-History mode ensures that sensitive strategy discussions, draft pleadings, and client instructions never leave your session memory — maintaining the confidentiality obligations that are fundamental to legal practice.

We will never comply with requests for client data without a valid court order, and we notify affected clients promptly (to the extent legally permitted) when any such request is received.
