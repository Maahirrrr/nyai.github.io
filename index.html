Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial available?+
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with full access, no credit card required. You can upgrade, downgrade or cancel at any time during the trial.
Is my client data safe with NyAI?+
Absolutely. NyAI operates under a Zero Retention policy by default — your queries and documents are processed in memory and never stored. All data is hosted on MeitY-empanelled infrastructure within India. Enterprise clients get their own private VPC with no data ever touching shared infrastructure.
Can I use NyAI for citations in court filings?+
NyAI's citations should always be verified against official SCC, Manupatra, or Gazette records before use in any court filing. NyAI is a research aid that significantly accelerates the process — but independent verification remains the advocate's responsibility under BCI professional conduct rules.
What Indian courts and statutes are covered?+
NyAI covers all Supreme Court judgments, all 25 High Courts, NCLAT, NCLT, SAT, ITAT, TDSAT, and major tribunals. Statutes include all Central Acts, the BNS/BNSS/BSA, Companies Act, IBC, Income Tax Act, GST laws, FEMA, SEBI regulations, and more. The corpus is updated weekly.
How does billing work for teams?+
Professional Chambers is billed per workspace (up to 5 users) at a flat monthly rate. Enterprise pricing is custom and based on number of seats, deployment type, and integration requirements. All plans can be billed monthly or annually (annual saves 20%).

Technical Architecture

Built for the sensitivity of legal data

Every NyAI Enterprise deployment runs in an isolated Virtual Private Cloud. No shared infrastructure, no shared models, no shared logs. Your data never touches another client's environment.

We use AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Encryption keys are managed in dedicated Hardware Security Modules and rotated quarterly. Access to production systems requires hardware MFA and is logged immutably.

Our models are hosted entirely on internal infrastructure. We do not route sensitive legal queries through any third-party AI provider including OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google. All inference happens within our controlled environment.

Annual red-team penetration testing is conducted by CERT-In empanelled security firms. We operate a responsible disclosure programme with a 48-hour response SLA for critical vulnerabilities.

Our Story

Why we built NyAI

India has over 1.7 million advocates and thousands of in-house legal teams. Yet the tools available to them were never designed for the unique complexity of Indian law — foreign-built software adapted for a system it fundamentally doesn't understand.

The Indian legal system is vast and deeply layered: 25 High Courts with distinct practices and local rules, a Supreme Court corpus spanning decades, hundreds of specialised tribunals, and a constantly evolving regulatory framework across SEBI, RBI, MCA, IRDAI, CCI, and more. International AI products simply don't understand this landscape.

NyAI was founded in 2024 by lawyers, AI researchers, and product builders from Ahmedabad who set out to change this. We built from first principles — curating Indian law datasets, training models on Indian legal reasoning, and designing workflows around how Indian practitioners actually work.

You remain solely responsible for the accuracy of any legal work product produced using NyAI.

2. No Attorney-Client Relationship

Use of NyAI does not create an attorney-client relationship between you and NyAI Technologies Pvt. Ltd. Our role is strictly limited to providing a research and drafting interface. Nothing in NyAI's outputs constitutes legal advice.

3. Bar Council of India Compliance

Users who are enrolled advocates are responsible for ensuring their use of NyAI complies with the Bar Council of India Rules of Professional Conduct and Etiquette. NyAI is a research tool only and may not be used to solicit clients or misrepresent AI-generated content as independent legal work.

Access is granted on a subscription basis for your lawful professional use. Accounts may not be shared across firms. Misuse for generating bulk content, scraping the corpus, or any non-legal purpose will result in immediate termination without refund.
