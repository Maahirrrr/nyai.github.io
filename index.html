The Story

Why We Built NyAI

India has over 1.7 million advocates and thousands of in-house legal teams. Yet the tools available to them — generic document editors, foreign-built legal software, or expensive manual research — were never designed for the unique complexity of Indian law.

The Indian legal system is vast and deeply layered: 25 High Courts with distinct practices, a Supreme Court corpus spanning decades, hundreds of tribunals, and a constantly evolving regulatory framework. International legal AI products simply do not understand this landscape.

NyAI was founded in 2024 by a team of lawyers, AI researchers, and product builders who wanted to change this. We built from first principles — curating Indian law data, training models on Indian legal reasoning, and designing workflows around how Indian legal professionals actually work.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect only the necessary data required to operate the NyAI platform. Your queries, uploaded documents, and generated responses are processed in-memory and are not retained by our servers after your session terminates, unless you explicitly opt-in to chat history retention.

2. Legal Privilege & Confidentiality

NyAI acts purely as an automated processor of your data. We do not use your proprietary prompts or uploaded legal documents to train our core foundation models, ensuring your attorney-client privilege is never compromised.

3. Security

All internet traffic to & from our application is encrypted via TLS 1.3 protocol. Data stored at rest is encrypted with AES-256. We undergo independent SOC 2 Type II audits annually.
