Research That Cites Its Sources

  • Automatic case law mapping and precedent hierarchy

Upload any agreement and NyAI reviews it against Indian law standards — flagging non-compliant clauses, missing protections, and negotiation leverage points.

  • One-click DD report in client-ready format

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Sovereignty

NyAI processes all query data in RAM. We do not store your proprietary legal prompts or sensitive client documents unless you explicitly opt-in to conversation history features. Your data is used only to generate your response and is purged upon session termination.

2. Training Policy

We do not use your data or the interactions you have with NyAI to train our core foundation models. Your professional expertise and client privilege remain exclusively yours.
