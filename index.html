Our Mission

The Indian legal system, with its 50 million pending cases and ancient yet evolving statutes, requires more than just generic AI. We believe that by providing Indian lawyers with tools tuned to the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), the Civil Procedure Code, and local High Court rules, we can accelerate the delivery of justice.

Why India First?

While global models struggle with the nuances of Indian "boiler-plate" or the hierarchical precedent of 25 different High Courts, NyAI is trained specifically on this data. We don't just translate law; we understand the Indian courtroom context.

Zero Retention Policy

By default, NyAI does not store your prompts or resulting data. Once your session ends, the data is purged from our systems. We do not use your proprietary work product to train our models.

Advocate-Client Privilege

NyAI is built to respect the professional secrecy requirements under Section 126 of the Indian Evidence Act (now reflected in the Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam). Our 'Privileged Vault' ensures that drafting for litigation is never stored or used in a way that waives this privilege.

Indian Data Residency

All data processing for our Indian enterprise clients occurs within MeitY-empanelled data centers within the territory of India, ensuring absolute sovereignty over legal data.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Fiduciary Responsibility

NyAI acts as a 'Data Processor' on behalf of the individual advocate or law firm (the 'Data Fiduciary'). In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, we process personal data only for providing legal research and drafting services.

2. Right to Erasure & Correction

Users have the right to request erasure of their research history and correction of any personal information. Our 'Zero Retention' mode ensures that no data persists beyond the active session.

3. Data Residency

All data is processed within the sovereign territory of India, utilizing MeitY-empanelled cloud infrastructure (Mumbai/Pune zones), ensuring compliance with Section 16 of the DPDP Act regarding cross-border transfers.

4. Information Security

We implement technical and organizational measures including AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of privileged legal information.

Terms of Service

1. Professional Responsibility

NyAI is a technological aid for legal professionals. It does not replace the requirement for an advocate's independent verification. Users must verify all citations against official Gazette notifications or SCC/Manupatra records before use in any court of law.

2. No Attorney-Client Relationship

Use of NyAI does not create an attorney-client relationship between the user and the platform providers. Our role is strictly limited to providing a research and drafting interface.

3. Subscription & Usage

Access is provided on a subscription basis. Misuse of the platform for generating bulk litigation or non-legal content may result in immediate suspension without refund, in line with Bar Council of India standards of professional conduct.
