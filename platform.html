Platform

The complete AI platform
for Indian legal practice

Purpose-built for the Indian legal system — trained on every court, statute, and regulatory body that matters. Not adapted. Not translated. Built from the ground up.

01 — Legal Research

Research That Cites Its Sources

Trained on the complete corpus of Supreme Court judgments, all 25 High Courts, NCLAT, SAT, ITAT, NCLT, and major regulatory bodies. Every answer is fully traceable.

  • Semantic search across SC, HCs, tribunals, and statutes
  • Automatic case law mapping and precedent hierarchy
  • Statutory cross-references including amendments and notifications
  • Conflicting bench detection and reconciliation summaries
  • Export citations in Bluebook, SCC, and AIR formats
02 — Contract Intelligence

Contract Review for Indian Boilerplate

Upload any agreement and NyAI reviews it against Indian law standards — flagging non-compliant clauses, missing protections, and negotiation leverage points automatically.

  • One-click risk scoring with clause-level explanations
  • Redline suggestions with track-changes export
  • FEMA, IT Act, and Companies Act compliance checks
  • Comparison against your firm's fallback positions
  • SHA, SPA, MoU, NDA, employment contract templates
03 — Due Diligence

M&A Due Diligence at Machine Speed

Upload data room documents and NyAI creates a structured DD report in minutes — identifying risk across corporate, tax, employment, IP, and regulatory dimensions.

  • Automated document classification and indexing
  • Issue-by-issue risk flagging with severity ratings
  • Indian M&A and PE deal-specific checklists
  • Cross-document inconsistency detection
  • One-click DD report generation in client-ready format
04 — Regulatory Compliance

Stay Ahead of Every Change

Real-time monitoring across SEBI, RBI, MCA, IRDAI, and CCI. Automated alerts, compliance gap analysis, and structured summaries — delivered before your clients ask.

  • Real-time regulatory circular and notification monitoring
  • Automated impact analysis for your practice areas
  • DPDP Act, FEMA, and Companies Act compliance coverage
  • BNS/BNSS/BSA comparative mapping for criminal law changes
  • Scheduled digests and custom alert thresholds
