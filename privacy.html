Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Fiduciary Responsibility

NyAI acts as a Data Processor on behalf of the individual advocate or law firm (the Data Fiduciary). In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, we process personal data only for providing legal research and drafting services as instructed by you.

2. Zero Retention Default

By default, no query, document, or response is retained after your session ends. Conversation history is only stored if you explicitly enable it in Settings. You may delete all stored data at any time.

3. Indian Data Residency

All data is processed within the sovereign territory of India on MeitY-empanelled cloud infrastructure (Mumbai/Pune AZs), ensuring compliance with Section 16 of the DPDP Act regarding cross-border transfer restrictions.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and erase any personal data we hold. Submit requests to [email protected]. We respond within 72 hours for standard requests and 24 hours for urgent matters.

5. Information Security

We implement AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, hardware MFA for all production access, and immutable audit logs. We hold SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001:2022 certifications.
